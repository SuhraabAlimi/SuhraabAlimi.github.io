Business Plan

Stefano Tower Defense HD

Identity

Tower Defense Game with multiple levels and towers with upgrades where the goal is to stop Stefano from finishing the track. There will be at least 5 different towers to choose from, each with 8 unique upgrades in order to make every game unique. Each level will have different Stefano attempting to pass you, so you must create your defenses accordingly and not let too many past, or you lose. When you complete all the levels, you will be greeted with a final, very hard level, which after completion, will unlock the final games to complete the hidden story expressed in the levels.

Problem

Children and teenagers want to play a fun game to pass the time that requires both knowledge and skill but has a simple concept. Without a fun game, these people of adolesence's happiness and joy would be significantly lower than with one. These ages of children are the learning ages, so they need to be able to learn problem solving skills, and gain the skill of fast reactions, which without these skills, could hinder ones ability to work or drive.

Our Solution

Stefano Tower Defense HD is a game designed for adolescenct people that is simple to start but hard to master. There will be many complex enemies that you must defeat, through quickly placing towers and upgrading them. Some towers excel at specific circumstances, while others are better at other ones, so knowing what tower to place at what time is key. Sometimes, the player will need to quickly remove towers and add others in order to survive, so quick wits and skill is almost certainly needed. Some levels' rounds will need to be planned out ahead of time, so while playing the game, players would gain skills in planning, as well as fast reactions and problem solving from the circumstances before.

Target Market

The target audience of this game is children and teenagers who are looking for a fun game to play to pass time that also builds skills such as problem solving, fast reaction speeds, and even planning.

The Competition

There are thousands of different tower defense games that could be classified as competition, but the most prominent one would be Bloons TD 6 by Ninja Kiwi. Bloons is a tower defense game with over 20+ unique towers each with over 60 different types. Although this game may seem to be better than anything we could make, we will be taking a different approach in the tower defense genre by branching out and having a story mode as the main mode, which Bloons clearly lacks. This new mode will allow Stefano Tower Defense HD to be able to compete equally with Bloons, though it will just take up a different approach in the same genre.

Revenue Streams

Stefano Tower Defense will be sold for computers as a story game for $2.99, and it will have a mobile port that will be free, though it will contain advertisements. These advertisements can be removed for a one time microtransaction of $0.99.

Marketing Activities

Stefano Tower Defense will have its own twitter, youtube, and instagram account, all advertising the game for both PC and Mobile. Advertisements on youtube and instagram will also be bought in order to spread the word of the game.

Expenses

As the group is working on Stefano Tower Defense HD voluntarily, the only expenses would be buying advertisements, as model, music, script, and UI creation will all be done by group memebers, though once income is generated, wages for group members will be discussed.

Team and Key Roles

Currently, the only one on this team is Suhraab Alimi, but when the group grows, we will have programmers, animators, modelers, idea creators, and development leads.

Milestones

Some milestones that are for the project is making a proper shop system, making each tower and all their paths, making UI that is easy to use and to look at, making a level system for prefab levels, and making enemy AI with waypoints, children, and lives. Some other milestones could be making the first dollar or having a net profit.
